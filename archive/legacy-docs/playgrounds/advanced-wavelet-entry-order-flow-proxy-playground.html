Proxy Features

OFProxyDelta

Closed-bar body efficiency, close location, and tick-volume surprise. Not executed buyer minus seller delta.

OFProxyAggression

Direction, volume anomaly, close location, and range quality. Not footprint imbalance at price.

OFProxyAbsorption

High effort with poor progress near structure, with wick/rejection context.

OFProxyStackedPressure

Consecutive same-side proxy pressure bars near structure.

OFProxyDivergence

Rolling closed-bar price extremes versus proxy pressure extremes.

OFProxyDecisionState

EA-derived proceed, investigate, or stand-down state relative to the base wavelet signal.
